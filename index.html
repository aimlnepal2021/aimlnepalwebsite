Why AIML Nepal?

No Place For Troubleshooting

Sep 3, 2022 I Physical Networking Event

Physical Event on Data Science 101

The event was aimed at guiding entry-level data science enthusiasts who wish to begin their data science journey from scratch and/or those who wish to integrate data science into their current professional roles. During the event, the speakers highlighted the role and applicability of data science, lifecycle of a data science project, proposed path and resources to become a data scientist, and finally, a notebook presentation of an intro-level project.
